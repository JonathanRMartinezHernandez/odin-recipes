Maple and Brown Sugar Oatmeal



Description

A nice alternative to the store-bought packets of Maple and Brown Sugar Oatmeal that can be adjusted perfectly to your liking!


Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of quick oats
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 & 1/2 cup cup of water

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil. Add oats and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in brown sugar and maple syrup. Let sit until desired thickness is reached, 2 to 3 minutes.
